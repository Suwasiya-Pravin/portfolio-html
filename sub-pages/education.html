My Education

Education Background:

1. Pre-School (Balvadi): I started my early education at Balvadi in Maharashtra, where I spent 2 months.

2. Primary Education (1st to 7th Std - BMC): After my time in Balvadi, I attended primary school in the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) from 1st to 7th standard.

3. 8th Std (Swami Vivekanand Kanishtha Mahavidyalya): I continued my education at Swami Vivekanand Kanishtha Mahavidyalya for 8th standard.

4. COVID-19 Gap Year: Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, I experienced a one-year gap in my education.

5. 9th to 10th Std (Gyan Vikas Hindi Night High School): After the gap year, I resumed my education at Gyan Vikas Hindi Night High School for 9th and 10th standard. I performed exceptionally well in 10th grade, scoring 90%.

6. 11th Std (Swami Vivekanand Kanishtha Mahavidyalya): I got admission to Swami Vivekanand Kanishtha Mahavidyalya in the 11th standard and chose the science stream with a focus on Computer Science (CS).
